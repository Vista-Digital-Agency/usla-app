Festivities

Parade of Athletes / Welcome Ceremony

All lifeguard and junior athletes, coaches and officials are invited to walk in the Championship Parade of Athletes. The parade will be led by the Beach Events All-Star Marching Band from 31st Street to the 4th Street Park for a short welcome ceremony. The Mayor of Virginia Beach, USLA President Peter Davis and a couple of others want to formally welcome you to the city and the 30th edition of the USLA National Lifeguard Championships.

Surf & Rescue Museum Happy Hour

Once the Welcome Ceremony is over, please take a moment to walk into the newly renovated Surf & Rescue Museum. Those who were here in the past will remember it as the Old Coast Guard Station, but now it is truly committed to the lifeguards and ocean rescue. Adults and competitors alike will enjoy the history of surf lifesaving in Virginia Beach with a section dedicated to the USLA.

Competitor Dinner

The VBLA is graciously sponsoring a free dinner for the athletes before the Primetime National Beach Flag Finals under the lights! Athletes will have their choice of two of the VB guards’ favorite restaurants, Beach Bully with their famous barbecue and Pelon’s excellent Mexican fare!

Awards Party @ Peabody’s

Ask anyone from 2014 and they will tell you when the competition ends, the party begins! And there is only one place that can host a party big enough for this crew. The actual trophy presentation to the winning teams will take place at 9:30pm sharp so make sure you have your sharpest party dress or outfit ready for a fun night of dancing and celebrating the 30th edition of the USLA National Lifeguard Championships!
